Equity Research: PayPal Holdings, Inc. (PYPL)

Executive Summary

This research evaluates PayPal's investment potential amidst a maturing digital payment market. Despite intensified competition from Apple Pay and Block, PayPal remains a highly profitable market leader with exceptionally strong cash flow generation.

Key Investment Thesis

Undervaluation: With a P/E ratio of approximately 12–13, the stock is trading significantly below its historical average and the broader technology sector.

Strong Fundamentals: PayPal maintains a massive user base and continues to grow revenue at a steady 4–6% annually.


Capital Allocation: The company recently initiated a quarterly dividend of $0.14 per share, signaling a shift toward returning value to shareholders.

Risk Assessment

Competition: Rising pressure from Apple Pay and Stripe.


Growth Deceleration: Revenue growth has slowed from double digits to mid-single digits as the sector matures.
